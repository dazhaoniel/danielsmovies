Back to the Future (1985)

Wed, Aug 23, 2017

Classics presented by Rooftop Cinema Club in association with Dow Jones, for Dow Jones employees only and I was lucky to attend. I guess back in 1985 Sci-Fi films are much simpler. Not a fan of the mix of Sci-Fi and comedy but the rooftop movie experience was nice.

Walk With Me (2017)

Fri, Aug 18, 2017

It’s interesting to see the monastery life of Buddhist monks and nuns, but enlightenment didn’t happen to me today. Maybe next time.

Stalker (1979)

Wed, Aug 16, 2017

First of all, how could this be a “Sci-Fi” film and be called “Stalker”? It is more accurate to be a poetry and named “Smuggler”. Considering it was made in the Soviet Union, I can see some of the monologues’ meanings. Great visual, music and sound effects. It’s apocalyptic, will leave you wondering for days.

Good Time (2017)

Tue, Aug 15, 2017

Probably the best movie of the year. This movie did everything right. Characters were very convincing. Excellent use of action and aerial shots and music. I’d willing to see it again next week.

Columbus (2017)

Fri, Aug 4, 2017

This movie has the potential to be so much better but didn’t. John Cho was a charming in the movie and I’d fall in love with him. Everything else sucks. Too slow, too boring, waste of time. The main actress was horrible. I know what it feels like to be a young girl having a crush on an older man, having been such a girl myself for years, and I can surely do a better job than her. I probably checked my phone for the most times during this movie than every other because it’s just too boring. Too bad because the story is actually interesting. The movie is more than 2 hours long, could trim to 1.5 hours and be a much better story.

Mal De Pierres (2016)

Thu, Aug 3, 2017

Also called “From the Land of the Moon” in English. Melodrama. Good story and imagination. Marion Cotillard’s performance is great as usual.

Desert Hearts (1985)

Mon, Jul 24, 2017

Pioneer Lesbian movie. Worth seeing if you are into the genre. The sex scene is a little too long and too revealing, maybe not good for a theater.

Valerian (2017)

Sun, Jul 23, 2017

Very typical Luc Besson movie with great imagination and shots. The story is weak but still enjoyable. With the special effects, it is very entertaining.

Dunkirk (2017)

Tue, Jul 18, 2017

I was disappointed probably because my hope was too high. Still a great movie, just not the best one of the year. Excellent usage of sound effects and analogue camera.

Lady Macbeth (2017)

Mon, Jul 17, 2017

Femme Fatale type of protagonist. Great plot and adaptation of the original novel. I like how she got away with three murders in the end. It’s refreshing.

War of Planet of Apes (2017)

Sun, Jul 16, 2017

Horrible. The story is too slow and I almost fell asleep. My date actually fell asleep, and he had seen the previous two.

My Cousin Rachel (2017)

Wed, Jun 14, 2017

The book was great but the movie was disappointing. Even Rachel Weisz didn’t save it. The story jumps too quickly from one scene to another, and I believe people who didn’t read the book couldn’t really understand it.

The Mummy (2017)

Tue, Jun 13, 2017

Probably (and hopefully) the worst Tom Cruise movie ever. Please, no more instalment of the mummy!

Alien: Covenant (2017)

Sun, May 21, 2017

Good story and special effects, it has all the elements to be a good Sci-Fi/Horror movie and be entertaining.

Guardians of Galaxy Vol. 2 (2017)

Sun, May 14, 2017

Super funny though the story is a little predictable that the dad is the villain. I would still want to see Vol. 3.

The Circle (2017)

Sun, May 7, 2017

Not as bad as I thought, but still I can do without seeing it. Emma Watson should just be a public figure and UN Ambassador and stop acting.

The Promise (2017)

Sun, Apr 30, 2017

A heavy topic like the Armenian Genocide should be based some better story than a love triangle and the woman eventually dies. It’s just too Hollywood. Disappointing.

Lost City of Z (2017)

Sun, Apr 23, 2017

Based on a true story, could be better. The title is confusing, it should be something suggesting that this is a story about a man’s quest for a lost world instead of a journey through an unknown culture/territory.

Life (2017)

Sun, Apr 9, 2017

A weird story indicating humanity was destroyed in the end. Most of the Sci-Fi movies are not as depressing as this one and for this reason, I like this one better.

Logan (2017)

Sun, Mar 5, 2017

My first X-Men movie of any series and probably the last one since Logan dies in the end. It’s a good story well told.

Get Out (2017)

Wed, Mar 1, 2017

Funny and creative. Too many people already talked too much about it so I won’t repeat.

A Cure for Wellness (2017)

Sun, Feb 19, 2017

The story is weird, also meaning creative. Probably one of the best movies of the year. Music is really good.

XXX: Return of Xander Cage (2017)

Sun, Feb 5, 2017

It seems like the producer picked actors first then found some random person to write the plot; if you can call that a plot. Hence there is no coherent story. It is a complete waste of time and money. Vin Diesel is a cool guy and I enjoy seeing his updates on Facebook Live, but these types of movies watching the trailer is enough.

Silence (2016)

Tue, Jan 10, 2017

Too long. The narrators at the beginning and the end are different so it was a little confusing. The filming location of rural Japan is amazingly beautiful though.

Live by Night (2017)

Mon, Jan 2, 2017

Very bad. Ben Affleck should think twice before sitting in the director’s chair again next time.

Patriots Day (2017)

Sun, Jan 1, 2017

Poster Design is great. Even though the movie had a star Mark Wahlberg, it did not turn into a one man hero narrative, instead, every character is well presented and has a distinctive personality. Also excellent use of actual footage and interviews of survivors in the end. It’s an inspiration whether or not you run marathons.
